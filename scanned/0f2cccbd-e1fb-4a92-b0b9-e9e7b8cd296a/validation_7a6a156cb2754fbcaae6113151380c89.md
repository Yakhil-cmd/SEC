### Summary

In the ICP ledger's ICRC-2 implementation, the `icrc2_approve` endpoint uniformly gates on `can_send` for the **caller** (the approver), including when the caller is attempting to **revoke** an existing allowance (amount = 0). Simultaneously, `icrc2_transfer_from` only checks `can_send` for the **caller** (the spender), never for the `from` account.
