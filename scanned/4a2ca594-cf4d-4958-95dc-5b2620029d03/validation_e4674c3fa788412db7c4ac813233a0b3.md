### Title
Deferred Resource-Allocation Charge Not Reflected in Ingress-Induction Balance Check During Paused (DTS) Execution - (File: rs/cycles_account_manager/src/cycles_account_manager.rs)

### Summary
When a canister is mid-execution under Deterministic Time Slicing (DTS), the scheduler intentionally defers resource-allocation charges to avoid mutating the balance during an unfinished operation. However, the balance guard used to accept new ingress messages during that same paused window — `debited_balance()` — does not subtract the accruing-but-deferred resource-allocation obligation. A canister can therefore accept ingress messages whose combined cost, once the deferred charge is finally collected, exceeds the actual available balance, leading to unexpected uninstallation or silent charge-dropping.
