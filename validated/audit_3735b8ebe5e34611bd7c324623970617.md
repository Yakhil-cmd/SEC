### Title
Unverified `ft_on_transfer` Amount Enables Unbacked ERC-20 Minting via Permissionless NEP-141 Registration — (File: `engine/src/engine.rs`)

---

### Summary

The `receive_erc20_tokens` function mints ERC-20 tokens based solely on the `amount` field supplied in the `ft_on_transfer` callback, without verifying that Aurora's actual NEP-141 balance increased by that amount. Because `deploy_erc20_token` has no access control, any unprivileged account can register a malicious NEP-141 and then call `ft_on_transfer` on Aurora directly — minting arbitrary, unbacked ERC-20 tokens and creating insolvency in the bridge accounting.
