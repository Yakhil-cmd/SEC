### Title
Missing Validation in `set_eth_connector_contract_account` Enables Temporary Freezing of User Withdrawals - (File: engine/src/contract_methods/connector.rs)

### Summary
`set_eth_connector_contract_account` in `engine/src/contract_methods/connector.rs` accepts any syntactically valid NEAR `AccountId` without verifying that the account exists on-chain or implements the required ETH connector interface. If the owner sets an incorrect account — a typo, a non-existent account, or an account that does not expose the expected methods — every downstream connector operation (`withdraw`, `ft_transfer`, `ft_transfer_call`, `storage_deposit`, `storage_unregister`, `storage_withdraw`) will create NEAR promises that silently fail, halting all bridging and token operations until the configuration is corrected.

### Finding Description
