### Title
Saturated `native_per_pubdata` and `native_prepaid_from_gas` Create Inconsistent Resource Accounting for L1 Transactions with Extreme Gas Prices - (File: `basic_bootloader/src/bootloader/transaction_flow/zk/process_l1_transaction.rs`)

---

### Summary

When an L1 transaction carries a gas price that overflows `u64` (i.e., `gas_price > u64::MAX * L1_TX_NATIVE_PRICE`), the `prepare_and_check_resources` function saturates both `native_per_gas` and `native_per_pubdata` to `u64::MAX`. These two saturated values are then used together in `create_resources_for_tx`, where the intrinsic pubdata overhead (`native_per_pubdata * L1_TX_INTRINSIC_PUBDATA`) also saturates to `u64::MAX`. The resulting native limit becomes `u64::MAX − u64::MAX = 0`, leaving the transaction with zero native resources. The transaction reverts even though the user deposited sufficient ETH on L1 to cover execution.
